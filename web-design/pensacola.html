What Makes PensacolaSites Different

Most web design agencies in Pensacola build on WordPress. It powers roughly 40% of the web, and it has its place. But for small businesses that need speed, security, and simplicity, WordPress introduces problems that most business owners never see until it is too late — slow load times from bloated plugins, security vulnerabilities that require constant patching, and monthly maintenance costs that add up year after year.

We take a different approach. Every website we build is hand-coded using clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Here is what that means for your business:

  • Page speed that Google rewards. Our sites consistently score 95 or higher on Google PageSpeed Insights. Speed is a direct ranking factor. A faster site means higher search rankings and fewer visitors bouncing before they see your content.
  • Zero plugin vulnerabilities. No WordPress means no plugins to update, no database to maintain, and no security patches to install. Your site just works, month after month.
  • Complete code ownership. You own everything. Your code, your domain, your hosting. No proprietary platforms, no lock-in contracts. If you ever want to move your site or hire a different developer, you can.
  • Built-in local SEO. Every page includes proper title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, schema markup, and mobile-first design. These are the technical foundations that help your site appear in Pensacola search results.
  • Mobile-first design. Over 75% of local searches happen on phones. Our sites are designed mobile-first — the small screen experience is never an afterthought.
